Eira

Eira is more than just a glove - it's a style statement. The clean lines, refined silhouette, and solid craftmanship make it an obvious choice for those who want to invest in something that lasts year after year. Whether you're heading to work, strolling through the city, or dressing up for an evening out, Eira is the perfect companion, adding both warmth and attitude.

Elevate your winter style with our premium leather gloves. Crafted for warmth, comfort, and timeless elegance, each pair is designed to fit perfectly while keeping your hands cosy.

Inspired by the Swedish forests

Founded by siblings Eldur and Eira, Skinsaga was founded in 1967 and is now run by the third and fourth generation of the Haidur family. Both design and development is done in a small village in Hälsingland, Sweden. Our gloves are manufactured in our own factories and we personally buy all leather, fabric, wool and other materials.
